Our Fall 2025 Competition will be held on November 8th at MIT, register here!

Competition Format

High school students and middle school students compete within their divisions; students can either register their own teams or be randomly assigned to one on the day of the competition.

Individual Round: 30 questions to be answered by the individual in 60 minutes

Relay Round 1: 4 questions per team, 10 minutes for the entire round

Relay Round 2: 4 questions per team, 10 minutes for the entire round

Team Round: 15 questions to be solved by the team in 60 minutes


A schedule of events will be posted closer to the day of the competition!
